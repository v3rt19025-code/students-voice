THE STUDENT'S VOICE

A Mental Health Awareness Project


Welcome to The Student’s Voice

A mental health awareness platform created to support students and give them a safe space to be heard.

Our Mission

Our goal is to raise awareness about mental health, provide helpful resources, and allow students to share their thoughts and experiences anonymously. Through our website, surveys, and activities, we aim to better understand student needs and promote a more open, supportive school environment where every voice matters.

What we've Done

We have conducted a comprehensive survey to gather insights on student mental health, we have done a class visit at our school to discuss the importance of mental health, and we are continuously working to improve our platform and resources based on the feedback we receive.
